Poster Design

URINETOWN

A theatrical project brief resulting in a finished poster outcome as well as flyer designs and clothing mockups.

My Notes

URINETOWN's project brief was a good starting point to learn more about poster compositions, but also practice new techniques like creating mockups of potential merchandise using the musical's logo I created for the final poster. This project came with many back and forth emails and many changes that had to be made, however it showed me the workflow of what a designer goes through and it taught me new techniques along the way as well. I am very proud of how great the final designs came out and I would love to take on another project like this in the future.
